## DBT-MIND APP INTEGRATION RULES

**The app mention must feel like:**
- An insider tip being shared within the community
- A tool the character naturally reaches for
- Something a caring person recommends
- A private coping mechanism

**The app mention must NOT feel like:**
- An advertisement
- A solution being pushed
- The point of the content
- Preachy or prescriptive

**Natural integration patterns:**

1. **Crisis grab:** "me opening DBT-Mind in the bathroom at 2am"
2. **Friend recommendation:** "me sending her the DBT-Mind app link for the 5th time"
3. **Private tool:** "nobody knows I use DBT-Mind to get through conversations"
4. **Skill delivery:** "the DBT-Mind app telling me to check the facts when I want to spiral"
5. **Growth moment:** "actually using the DBT-Mind app instead of spiraling this time"

**Placement:** Typically slide 5 (near end but not the finale), occasionally slide 3-4 if it fits the narrative flow. NEVER slide 1 or the final slide.

**Total slides:** 6 (optimal for engagement and pacing)

**Use:**
- Lowercase (tiktok native style)
- Minimal punctuation except for effect

**Avoid:**

- Diagnostic criteria framing

- Hopeless endings without solidarity

- Assume the viewer already knows the terminology
- Inside jokes > explanations

## CONTENT SAFETY GUIDELINES

**Always:**
- Frame BPD experiences with compassion
- Show coping and growth alongside struggle
- Include moments of connection and love
- End on notes of hope, solidarity, or gentle humor

**Never:**
- Glorify or romanticize self-harm or crisis
- Present BPD as inherently abusive or toxic
- Create content that could be used to mock people with BPD
- Include specific methods of self-harm
- Frame untreated BPD as acceptable ("I don't need help")

**Sensitive topics approach:**
- Suicidal ideation: Only reference obliquely if at all ("the dark thoughts"), never detail
- Self-harm: Can acknowledge existence without specifics
- Substance use: Can mention as coping, not glorify
- Relationship abuse: Never frame BPD behaviors as justified abuse
